Theorem 7.2.14.

If \(\ell \) is a prime not equal to the residue characteristic of \(K\text{,}\) then

  1. \(a_G\) and \(\mathrm{sw}_G\) are realisable over \(\QQ_\ell\)
  2. There exists a f.g. projective left \(\ZZ_\ell \lb G\rb\)-module \(\mathrm{Sw}_G\) unique up to iso. such that
    \begin{equation*} \mathrm{Sw}_G \otimes \ZZ_\ell \QQ_\ell \end{equation*}
    is isomorphic to the Swan representation.

reference: Serre, Linear representations of finite groups.
