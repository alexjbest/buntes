Proposition 3.2.5
  1. Preimage resistant iff when given \(j\) it is difficult to compute a positive integer \(e\) and an isogeny \(\phi\colon E_{j_0} \to E_j\) with degree \(l^e\text{.}\)
  2. Collision resistant iff when given \(j\) it is difficult to compute \(e\) and \(\phi \colon E_{j_0} \to E_{j_0}\) with degree \(l^e\text{.}\)
