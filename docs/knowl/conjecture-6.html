Conjecture 7.1.14. Abhyankar's Inertia Conjecture.

Let \(G\) be a finite quasi-p group. Let \(G_{0}\) be a subgroup of \(G\) which is an extension of a cyclic group of order prime-to-p by a p-group \(G_{1}\text{.}\) Then \(G_{0}\) occurs as an inertia group for a \(G\) - Galois cover of the projective line branched only at \(\infty \) if and only if the conjugates of \(G_1\) generate \(G\)
