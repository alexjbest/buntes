Let \(G,H\) be fppf abelian sheaves. And set \(G_0, H_0\) the corresponding objects restricted to \(R_0\text{.}\) Suppose

  1. \(G\) is \(p\)-divisible.
  2. \(\hat H\) is formal.
  3. \(H (R) \to H(R/J)\) surjective for any nilpotent \(J\) (this is known as formal smoothness of \(J\))

then

  1. Both
    \begin{equation*} \Hom (G,H),\,\Hom(G_0,H_0) \end{equation*}
    are \(p\)-torsion free.
  2. The reduction mod \(I\)
    \begin{equation*} \Hom(G, H) \to \Hom(G_0, H_0) \end{equation*}
    is injective.
  3. For \(f_0 \in \Hom(G_0,H_0)\text{,}\) there is a unique \(\Phi (G, H)\) with
    \begin{equation*} \Phi \equiv N^\nu f_0 \mod I \end{equation*}
    denote \(\Phi = \tilde N^\nu f \in \Hom(G,H) \otimes \QQ\)
  4. we get
    \begin{equation*} f = \frac{\tilde N^\nu f}{N^\nu } \in \Hom (G,H) \end{equation*}
    \begin{equation*} \iff \end{equation*}
    \begin{equation*} \tilde N^\nu (G[N^\nu ] ) = 0 \end{equation*}
