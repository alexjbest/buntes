Theorem 5.3.11.

\(J(X) \) is the \(\CC\) points of an abelian variety over \(\CC\text{.}\) Further the map

\begin{equation*} \Pic^0(X) \to J(X) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} [P] - [Q] \mapsto (\omega \mapsto \int^P_Q \omega) \end{equation*}

is an isomorphism of abelian groups.

Proof.
