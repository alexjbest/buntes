Let \(x,x'\in X_0(N)\) be non-cuspidal

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}\langle(x)\amp\left.-(\infty),\left(x^{\prime}\right)-(0)\right\rangle_{\mathbb{C}} \\ \amp=\lim _{s \rightarrow 1}\left[G_{N, s}\left(z, z^{\prime}\right)+4 \pi E_{N}\left(w_{N} z, s\right)+4 \pi E_{N}\left(z^{\prime}, s\right)+\frac{\kappa_{N}}{s-1}\right]+C \end{aligned}\text{.} \end{equation*}

This can be re-stated as

\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned}\amp\langle(x)-(\infty), T_{m}\left(\left(x^{\prime}\right)-(0)\right)\rangle_{\mathbb{C}} \\=\amp \lim _{s \rightarrow 1}\left[G_{N, s}^{m}\left(z, z^{\prime}\right)+4 \pi \sigma_{1}(m) E_{N}\left(w_{N} z, s\right)\right.\\ \amp\left.+4 \pi m^{s} \sigma_{1-2 s}(m) E_{N}\left(z^{\prime}, s\right)+\frac{\sigma_{1}(m) \kappa_{N}}{s-1}\right]-\sigma_{1}(m) \lambda_{N}+2 \sigma_{1}(m) \kappa_{N} \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
