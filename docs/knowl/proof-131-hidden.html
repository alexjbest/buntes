\begin{equation*} \Hom_W (\underline x^\sigma ,\underline x) = \Hom_{W/\pi ^n}(\underline x^\sigma , \underline x) \end{equation*}

for all \(n\text{.}\) As \(r_{\mathcal A}(m)= 0\) we have no elements of degree \(m\text{.}\)
