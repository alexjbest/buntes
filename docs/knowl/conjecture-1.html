Conjecture 2.9.3 ABC

Let \(A,B,C\in \ZZ\) s.t. \(\gcd(A,B,C) =1 \) and \(A+B+C=0\text{,}\) then for all \(\epsilon \gt 0\) there exists a constant \(k_\epsilon\) s.t.

\begin{equation*} N(A,B,C) \gt k_\epsilon H(A,B,C)^{1-\epsilon} \end{equation*}

where

\begin{equation*} N(A,B,C) = \prod_{p|ABC} p \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} H(A,B,C) = \max(|A|,|B|,|C|)\text{.} \end{equation*}
