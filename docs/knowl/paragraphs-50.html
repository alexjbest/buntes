History of the proof.
  • Fermat (~ 1640) proved \(FLT(4)\text{.}\)
  • Euler (~ 1758-1770) proved \(FLT(3)\text{.}\)
Note 6.1.2.

Since \(FLT(d) \implies FLT(n)\) whenever \(d|n \) it suffices to prove \(FLT(p)\) for all primes \(p \ge 5\text{.}\)

The proof uses the rich arithmetic theory of elliptic curves, modular forms and Galois representations. We emphasise the contributions of
