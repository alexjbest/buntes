Theorem 3.3.17

Over a local field \(F \ne \CC\) there is a unique division quaternion algebra \(B/F\) up to \(F\)-isomorphism.

If \(F = \QQ_p\text{,}\) \(p\ne 2\) then this is

\begin{equation*} \legendre{e,p}{\QQ_p} \end{equation*}

for \(e\) any quadratic non-residue mod \(p\text{.}\)

This is saying that any quadratic extension of \(F\) embeds into \(B\text{!}\)
