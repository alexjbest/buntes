Theorem 5.7.9.

Let \(G_1\) be the smallest subgroup of \(G\) through which all the \(h \in X\) factor. A faithful representation \(V\) is polarizable iff

  1. \(G_1\) is reductive.
  2. For some \(h \in X\) (equivalently for all \(h \in X\)) conjugation by \(h(i)\) is a Cartan involution on the adjoint group \(G_1^{\ad}\text{.}\)
