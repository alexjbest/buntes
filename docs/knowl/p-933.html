Let \(\QQ_{p,\infty}\) be the unique quaternion algebra ramified at \(p,\infty\text{,}\) let \(q \equiv 3 \pmod 4\) be such that \(\legendre pq = -1\text{,}\) then

\begin{equation*} \QQ_{p,\infty} \cong \begin{cases} \legendre{-1,-1}{\QQ} \amp \text{ if }p\equiv 2\pmod 4,\\ \legendre{-1,-p}{\QQ} \amp \text{ if }p\equiv 3\pmod 4,\\ \legendre{-2,-p}{\QQ} \amp \text{ if }p\equiv 1\pmod 8,\\ \legendre{-p,-q}{\QQ} \amp \text{ if }p\equiv 5\pmod 8. \end{cases} \end{equation*}
