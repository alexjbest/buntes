Observation 2.11.12

\(\PP^1 \smallsetminus \{0,1,\infty\}\) is the moduli space of genus 0 curves with four (ordered) marked points.

\begin{equation*} (\PP^1, \alpha_1,\alpha_2,\alpha_3,\alpha_4) \mapsto \image(\alpha_4) \text{ when } \alpha_1 \mapsto 0,\alpha_2\mapsto 1, \alpha_3 \mapsto \infty\text{.} \end{equation*}
