Proposition 2.6.19. Criterion 3.29.

For a compact Riemann surface \(S\) the following are equivalent

  1. \(S\) is defined over \(\overline \QQ\text{.}\)
  2. \(\{S^\sigma\}_{\sigma \in \operatorname{Gal}(\CC)}\) contains only finitely many isomorphism classes of Riemann surfaces.
