Theorem 1.9.5. Riemann-Roch.

Let \(A\) be an abelian variety of dimension \(g\) then

  1. The degree of \(\lambda_{\sheaf L}\) is \(\chi(\sheaf L)^2\text{.}\)
  2. If \(\sheaf L = \sheaf L(D)\) then \(\chi(\sheaf L) = (D^g)/g!\text{,}\) this is the \(g\)-fold self intersection number of \(D\text{.}\)
