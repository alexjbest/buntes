Proposition 2.9.8.

Let \(K\) be a number field an d \(C/K\) a curve. Let \(f \in K(C)\) be a rational function of degree \(d\text{.}\) Then for \(p \in C(K) \smallsetminus f\inv(0)\) we have

\begin{equation*} \log N_0(f(p)) \lt (1- b_f(0)/d) \log H(f(p)) + O(\sqrt{\log H(f(p))} + 1) \end{equation*}

with the following notation

\begin{equation*} N(r) =N_0(r) N_1(r) N_\infty(r) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} N_0(r) = \prod_{\ideal p \supseteq (r)} \norm(\ideal p) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} N_1(r) = \prod_{\ideal p \supseteq (1-r)} \norm(\ideal p) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} N_\infty(r) = \prod_{\ideal p \supseteq (1/r)} \norm(\ideal p) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} b_f(0) = \sum_{f(p) = 0} (e_p - 1)\text{.} \end{equation*}
Proof.
