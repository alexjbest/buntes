Theorem 6.4.1.
\begin{equation*} g_A(z) = \sum_{m\ge 1} \pair c {T_m c^\sigma } e^{2\pi i m z} \end{equation*}

is a cusp form of weight 2 on \(\Gamma _0(N)\) and satisfies

\begin{equation*} (f,g_A) = \frac{u^2 |D|^{\frac 12} L'_A(f,1)}{8\pi ^2} \end{equation*}

for all \(f\) in the space of newforms of weight 2 in \(\Gamma _0(N)\text{.}\)
